HOW TO MINT GUIDE

Step 1:

-Click on the "MINT" button on the main page of our website

Step 2:

-Click on "Connect to Web3"

Step 3:

-Click on "7.mintTiger"

Step 4:

-In the appeared window, in the "mintTiger" row, indicate the cost of the total amount of tigers you want to buy. For example: the cost of 1 tiger is 0.04ETH, the cost of 10 tigers is 0.4ETH.

-In the count "uint256" row, specify the number of tigers (maximum 20 per transaction)

Step 5:

-Confirm your transaction in Metamask
